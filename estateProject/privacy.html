Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to DJ Rentals. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you submit a rental application through our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a rental application, we collect the following information:

  • Full name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Current address and occupation
  • Financial information including monthly income
  • Rental history including previous landlord information
  • Personal background information relevant to tenancy
  • Uploaded identification documents
  • Information about additional occupants

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and evaluate your rental application
  • To contact you regarding your application status
  • To verify your identity and rental eligibility
  • To communicate updates about available properties

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your information is transmitted securely through EmailJS encryption. We do not sell, trade or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your application details are only accessible to authorized DJ Rentals staff involved in the rental process.

4. Information Storage

Application data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to process your rental application. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

5. Cookies

Our website uses local browser storage to temporarily save your application progress. No tracking cookies or third party analytics are used on this website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw your application at any time
